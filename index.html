About Us

Kidegembye Secondary School is a government institution established in 2005, located in Kidegembye Village within Njombe District Council in the Southern Highlands of Tanzania. The school benefits from a refreshing climate and scenic surroundings characterized by pine trees. Currently, it accommodates 520 students in its hostel facilities, providing them with quality education and care. Due to its strong academic performance, the school continues to attract new learners, with about 50 students recently joining. Kidegembye Secondary School remains committed to nurturing excellence and holistic development.
